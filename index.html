Also Researched

Themes where we found 1-2 strong anchors but not enough depth for a full 5-blog pack. Could work as smaller 3-blog packs or combined packs.

Language & Words

Sentence First (24,308 subs, 4 posts in 2026) by linguist Stan Carey. Strong anchor, but Dialect Blog is no longer active. Needs 2+ more language/linguistics blogs to fill a pack.

Film & Cinema

The Wonderful World of Cinema (524 subs, 15 posts in 2026) covers classic film. Paul's Movie Reviews (89 subs, 48 posts in 2026) is prolific. Low subscriber counts but active writers.

Personal Essays

Robert Brancatelli (311 subs) and Maybe It's Just Me are quality individual blogs. Hard to fill 5 without diluting quality.

Lifestyle & Wellness

Half Crazy Mama (54 subs, 3 posts in 2026) is barely active. Mental Health Matters (2,047 subs) has not posted since July 2025. Category needs fresh candidates.

Fashion & Design

Polyvore (4,221 subs) leads the category. Thin beyond the top 2-3 blogs.

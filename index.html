Also Researched (Didn't Make Top 6)

Language & Words

Sentence First (24,308 subs) by linguist Stan Carey is an exceptional anchor. Need 4 more language blogs to fill a pack.

Science & Tech

Promega Connections (7,764 subs, 27K views) and Out of Office Hours (1,948 subs, 28K views) are strong IT/biotech anchors.

Personal Essays

Robert Brancatelli (311 subs) and Maybe It's Just Me are great individual blogs but hard to fill 5.

Lifestyle & Wellness

Half Crazy Mama (7,508 subs) and Mental Health Matters (1,793 subs) lead a scattered category.

Film & Cinema

Active reviewers exist but subscriber counts are low. Top blog has 2,410 subs. Better as a niche pack than a general one.

Fashion & Design

Polyvore (4,221 subs) leads the category. Thin beyond the top 2-3 blogs.

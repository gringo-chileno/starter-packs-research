## 5. Graceful Little Honey Bee (gracefullittlehoneybee.com)

| Dimension | Score | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Content Quality | 3 | Standard budget/slow cooker recipes. Nothing distinctive. |
| Reader Appeal | 3 | Practical for budget-conscious families. Adequate but unremarkable. |
| Brand Safety | 5 | No content issues. |
| Fediverse Fit | 2 | Some personal voice in RSS feed ("I love making soup in my slow cooker") but the on-site experience buries personality under monetization infrastructure. |
| Visual Polish | 1 | Extremely ad-heavy (AdThrive with aggressive placement: sidebar, content, recipe, below-post, footer, sticky). Multiple overlapping ad units. Page content obscured by ad code. Not a site you'd proudly showcase. |
| **Total** | **14** | **Weak. Below threshold.** |

**Verdict: Replace.** The ad saturation makes this a poor showcase for WordPress. Even though the RSS feed shows genuine personal voice, the site experience is cluttered and ad-choked. Not the look we want when featuring WordPress blogs.
